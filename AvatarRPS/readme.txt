Rock-Paper-Scissors Avatar Version

Game Play
Enter/ play an element from the avatar cycle (air, water, earth, fire).
The computer will, at random, select from the same options to play against user.
If the selections are the same it will result in a draw, if they are polar opposites it will result in a zen moment.
The player or computer will win if the elements chosen come directly before the other in the avatar cycle.
For a win: air > water, water > earth, earth > fire, fire > air
For a zen moment: air = earth, water = fire

Please enjoy!
